Why use Clean Paste?

When you copy text from Word, Google Docs, Outlook, or a website, it brings invisible formatting — fonts, font sizes, colors, and layout styles hidden in HTML tags. Pasting into a CMS, email tool, or plain text field often breaks the layout or looks wrong.

Clean Paste shows you exactly what formatting was found and lets you pick what to strip — so you stay in control instead of losing everything at once.

How it works

Paste your text into the left box — bold, colors, and other formatting will show so you can see what's there. TextMop detects what it found and shows only the relevant cleanup options below. Check the ones you want, and the clean result appears instantly on the right.

Your text stays private

Everything happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Nothing you paste is ever sent to a server, saved, or logged.
