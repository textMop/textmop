Why use Clean Paste?

When you copy text from Word, Google Docs, Outlook, or a website, it brings invisible formatting with it — fonts, sizes, colors, and layout styles hidden in HTML. Pasting into a CMS or email tool often breaks your layout.

Clean Paste shows you what formatting was found and lets you pick exactly what to remove, so you stay in control.

Your text stays private

Everything happens locally in your browser. Nothing you paste is ever sent to a server, saved, or logged — safe for confidential documents and client work.
